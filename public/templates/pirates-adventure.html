The Captain's Code

1. Every man has a vote in affairs of moment; has equal title to fresh provisions, or strong liquors.

2. No man to game at cards or dice for money.

3. The lights and candles to be put out at eight o'clock at night.

4. To keep their piece, pistols, and cutlass clean and fit for service.

- Bartholomew Roberts
